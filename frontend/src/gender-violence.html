Types of Gender-Based Violence

Gender-based violence includes physical, emotional, sexual, and economic abuse. Here are some examples:

  • Physical violence: Inflicting harm through physical force.
  • Emotional abuse: Manipulation, control, and humiliation.
  • Sexual violence: Non-consensual sexual acts.
  • Economic abuse: Controlling finances and resources.
